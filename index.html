    PROVA PROVA PROVA¶

    Research

    Make your research more reproducible by writing a Python script or `Jupyter notebook`_ instead of clicking through complicated menus.

    Development

    Don’t start from scratch! Build upon the existing tools in Serendipyty to develop new methods.

    Teaching

    Combine Serendipyty with the `Jupyter notebook`_ to make rich, interactive documents. Great for teaching fundamental concepts of geophysics!
